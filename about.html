Our Story

From one cab in Rajkot to Gujarat's go‑to travel partner

Kuldevi Krupa Cab started in Rajkot with a simple idea — give travellers a cab service that's honest about pricing and serious about safety. What began with a single sedan has grown into a fleet of sedans, SUVs and tempo travellers serving 50+ cities across Gujarat.

Today, over 12,000 riders have travelled with us for office commutes, family yatras, airport transfers and long outstation trips. Every booking still gets the same promise we started with: a clean AC cab, a verified driver, and a fare that doesn't change at the destination.
